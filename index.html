I. The Three Arcs

how 16 chapters cohere
Arc 1

Foundations & Behavioural Insight

Chs. 1 — 4

Ethical theory grounded in established norms — utilitarianism, deontology, virtue ethics, fiduciary principles. Then the psychological and behavioural perspectives: moral disengagement, ethical fading, overconfidence. Why misconduct can occur even among well-intentioned professionals.

Arc 2

Regulatory Architecture & Specific Risks

Chs. 5 — 11

From historical regulatory development (Securities Act, IAA, Dodd-Frank) through specific ethical concerns: misconduct, risk mitigation, client-facing roles, insider trading, market manipulation, data privacy, and fair treatment of clients.

Arc 3

Firm Culture & Technology Adoption

Chs. 12 — 16

Ethical firm culture as strategic imperative beyond compliance. Five core technologies (AI/ML, RegTech, DLT, Open Banking, Cloud/SaaS). The SR 11-7 control loop. Three capstone cases that integrate everything.

III. About this Companion

This interactive companion turns each textbook chapter into a five-tab learning environment: Concept Map (with expandable details), Frameworks & Cases (the chapter's anchor cases), Knowledge Check (quiz with progress tracking), Case Workshop (discussion-guide scenarios with auto-saving textareas), and a chapter-specific Identifier (drag-or-tap matching exercise).

Quiz progress and workshop responses save automatically to your browser's local storage. They are private to your device and never transmitted anywhere. Clearing your browser data will reset them.
